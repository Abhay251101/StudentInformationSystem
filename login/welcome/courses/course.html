Courses offered by us

MCA - Master of Computer Applications

The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is a postgraduate degree program that focuses on providing a strong theoretical foundation and comprehensive understanding of various aspects of computer applications.

MSc - Master of Science

The Master of Science (MSc) programs are designed to provide in-depth knowledge in specific scientific or technical fields. Students can choose from a variety of specializations such as Physics, Chemistry, Biology, etc.

PhD - Doctor of Philosophy

The Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) is the highest academic degree that can be awarded to individuals who have made a significant contribution to their field of study through original research.

B.Tech - Bachelor of Technology

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) is a four-year undergraduate degree program that focuses on practical application, problem-solving, and technical skills in various engineering disciplines such as Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, etc.

BBA - Bachelor of Business Administration

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a three-year undergraduate program that provides a strong foundation in business and management principles.

BCA - Bachelor of Computer Applications

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a three-year undergraduate program that focuses on computer applications and software development.
